# Kunanon Burathep
**(Pronunciation: Ku-na-non Bu-ra-thep)**

**PhD Student** Department of Computer Science, Durham University

---

## About Me
I am a PhD student at Durham University, funded by an [EPSRC Doctoral Studentship][epsrc-link]. 

I am a member of the following research groups:
* [NESTiD][nestid-link] (Network Engineering, Science and Theory in Durham)
* [ACiD][acid-link] (Algorithms and Complexity in Durham)

My research focuses on **learning-augmented online algorithms**, with a specific focus on online bipartite matching. I also have a strong interest in **approximation algorithms** for graph connectivity problems.

---

## Research Interests
* **Learning-Augmented Online Algorithms**
  * Online Bipartite Matching
* **Approximation Algorithms**
  * Graph Connectivity Problems
